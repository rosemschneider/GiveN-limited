Give-N app

Start a test

Provide child with counting objects and a plate.

Say: "We’re going to play a game with these toys and this plate!
I want you to put toys on the plate – listen carefully!"

"Can you put N on the plate? (where N is the number word)
Put N on the plate and tell me when you’re all done." (repeat if the child does not respond).

When child tells you that they are done:

"Is that N?
Can you count and make sure?
Is that N?"

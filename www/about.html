About

The Give-N App was designed by the UCSD Language & Development Lab in an effort to standardize and make the Give-N task more accessible to researchers and parents. The logic of the app is based on code written by Katie Wagner. The initial website and database were designed and implemented by Jim Wright, with additional contributions from Jeffrey Wu. Thank you to Kevin Hughes for his work on the server.

Thank you for using the "Give-a-Number" app. This app deploys two standard methods for assessing children's understanding of number words drawn from the scientific literature. It estimates the numbers a child understands (between 1 and 10, or higher). The app provides trial-by-trial instructions for testing, generates an estimate of the child's knowledge at the end of the test, and emails results to the user.

Confidentiality: All data collected used this app will be stored on secure servers housed by the University of California, San Diego (UCSD). No identifying information regarding individual children will be stored. Note that anonymized recorded behaviors will be accessible to researchers at UCSD and may be shared with the wider research community.
